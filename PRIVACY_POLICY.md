# Privacy Policy for VisionTime

**Last Updated:** January 5, 2026

## Overview

VisionTime ("the App") is a personal entertainment tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Your tracked content**: Shows, movies, watch progress, ratings, and notes you add
- **App preferences**: Settings like notification preferences and display options

### What We DON'T Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or tracking
- Advertising identifiers

## Data Storage

All your data is stored:
1. **Locally** on your device
2. **In your private iCloud account** (if iCloud is enabled)

We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's iCloud infrastructure ensures your data remains private and encrypted.

## Third-Party Services

### The Movie Database (TMDb)
We use TMDb's public API to display information about movies and TV shows. When you search for content:
- Your search queries are sent to TMDb's servers
- TMDb's privacy policy applies to their service: [themoviedb.org/privacy-policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy)

### iCloud
If enabled, your data syncs via Apple's iCloud. Apple's privacy policy applies: [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your data
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your behavior

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete your data** by removing the app (local data) and disabling iCloud sync
- **Export your data** through iOS/visionOS backup features
- **Disable sync** by turning off iCloud for the app in Settings

## Children's Privacy

VisionTime does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: [support@dubikes.com]
